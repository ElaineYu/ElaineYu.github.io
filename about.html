About Me


Hello! My name is Elaine Yu and I’m a budding software developer. Recently I graduated from DevBootcamp, an intensive 9-week web developer apprenticeship on steroids. Currently I'm looking to join a team of bright, social developers at a tech company as a junior web developer.

Why learn to code? Because I want to make beautiful, meaningful products and help organizations investigate and solve big challenges through technology.

My professional career has been colorful and checkered, from English teacher in China, to diplomatic outreach assistant for a United Nations environmental treaty, to biofuel researcher. As a trained researcher and a continuous learner, I have an insatiable curiosity and a love for science.

In my free time , I’m deeply interested in helping open government data initiatives nurture digital products and bring data to life, which makes me excited to learn languages and libraries like R and d3.js. I’m also adventurous in travel and cooking, love standup-comedy and am learning how to surf.
